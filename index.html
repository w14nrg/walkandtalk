MENd: Walk and Talk for Men's Mental Health

A Community Interest Company (CIC) in West London (Hammersmith & Fulham, Kensington & Chelsea) offering free one-on-one walk and talk sessions in local parks. For any man—abuse, loss, addiction, or just need a rant and coffee. Led by men who’ve been there, because talking saved my life.

About Me: Why I Started MENd

I’m the founder of MENd, and this is just the beginning—it’s just me for now, but I’m looking for other men to join as peer leaders. My life hasn’t been easy: I survived childhood abuse, got kicked out of schools, nearly died in a bike crash, and cared for my father until he passed. I served time in prison, battled drug and alcohol addiction, struggled with toxic relationships, and even attempted suicide. Therapy helped, but what truly saved me was real, raw conversations—talking with someone who got it. That’s why I started MENd: to walk shoulder-to-shoulder with men in West London parks, no judgment, no stigma. Whether you’re dealing with trauma, grief, or just need to vent over a coffee, I’m here. Let’s walk, talk, and heal together.
